Getting started

Once you have joined the network, a P2P powered distribution is a few only clicks away:

1. Download the Vlynt Network Adapter into your website's folder.
2. Insert a single line of code into your index file.
3. Done!

Your web service is now powered by the Vlynt Network!


Why join?

Boost your performance

The Vlynt network finds the fastest route for every user.

Be it a peer or a server.

Reduce your distribution costs

When used in combination with a CDN or a central system, all data delivered through the P2P network effectively avoids the servers.

This could save you millions in bandwidth costs.

Infinite scalability

The Vlynt Network automatically scales as your userbase growths, both quantitatively and geographically.

This allows for a truly global reach, far beyond any existing servers.

Stay lean

Delivering data through the Vlynt Network is the cheapest solution for worldwide content distribution to this date.

How? Well, we don't need to pay for any servers...

Go Green

Effectively reduce your impact on climate change by bypassing servers.

No more need to build server-farms to heat up the artic.
